    Wednesday, Oct 30th 6-8PM Broida 3302

    Events

    Fall 2019


    Wednesday October 31st, 3:00-5:00 PM
    As the quarter sets in and midterms are nearly upon us, Undergraduate Diversity and Inclusion in Physics would like to invite all Freshman and Transfer students to a social this upcoming Wednesday, October 23rd from 3:00 - 5:00 PM on the Chem lawn in front of Broida Hall. There will be free ice cream and other treats, activities, and friend-making galore! Come out and meet some of your classmates as well as get to know the clubs within the Physics department, including demos by Physics Circus! This event is catered towards new students in the department, but if you are a returning student who would like to introduce yourself to the new students in the department you are more than welcome to come. We hope to see you all there!

    Questions? Email now!


    Wednesday October 30th, 6:00-8:00 PM


    Hey Frightening Physicists! Make sure to join us Wednesday, October 30 for the Spooky Imposter Syndrome Panel and the subsequent Spooky Social! UDIP is hosting a Spooky Imposter Syndrome Panel from 6-7pm in Broida 3302. Come to figure out what imposter syndrome is, why it affects so many of us, and what we can do to overcome it. There will be a panel of physics students and faculty to discuss their own experiences as well as answer audience questions. This will be followed by the Spooky Social, from 7-8pm in Broida 3302. Continue the discussion, or rise from your graves and CONSUME!!! (pizza and candy). Meet and treat with other physics majors, form study groups, or just chillax before or after finals! This event is cohosted by SPS and UDIP


    Wednesday, May 22nd 6:00-8:00 PM

    Do you have plans for after college? Questions about graduate school? Going into Industry? Totally lost? Want to discuss these questions in an informal, friendly setting with UCSB graduate students who have been through it all? Just want to get to know some other grads or undergrads? Then come to the UDIP grad-undergrad student mixer! On Wednesday, May 22 in in front of Broida at the picnic tables, from 6:00 to 8:00 pm, there will be a mixer for graduate and undergraduate students. This is an excellent opportunity to meet and mingle with the brightest minds UCSB has to offer! There will be activities and Free Food!
